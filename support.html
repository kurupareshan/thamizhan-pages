Frequently Asked Questions

Is Thamizhan free to use?

Yes, Thamizhan is completely free. All features including devotional songs, calendar, horoscope, and video maker are available at no cost.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Thamizhan requires no registration or account. Simply download and use it immediately.

How accurate is the panchanga and nakshatra data?

The panchanga calculations follow traditional Tamil Panchangam methods. For critical religious or astrological decisions, we recommend cross-referencing with a local jothidar (astrologer).

Why does the app ask for location permission?

Location is used optionally to provide accurate local panchanga timings and sunrise/sunset information for your area. You can deny this permission and enter your city manually instead.

The video export is not working. What should I do?

Make sure you have granted Thamizhan access to Photos in iOS Settings → Thamizhan → Photos. Video export requires a physical iPhone — it does not work on the simulator. Also ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device.

The devotional songs are not playing. What should I check?

Song streaming requires an active internet connection. Please check your Wi-Fi or mobile data. If the issue persists, close and reopen the app.

How do I use the marriage matching (Porutham) feature?

Go to the Porutham section, enter the boy's and girl's birth star (nakshatra). The app will calculate the 10 traditional poruthams and display the compatibility result.

Which iOS version does Thamizhan support?

Thamizhan requires iOS 12.0 or later. For the best experience, we recommend iOS 16 or newer.

Is my data stored on any server?

No. All personal data you enter (birth details, names) is stored only on your device. We do not upload or store any personal information on external servers.
